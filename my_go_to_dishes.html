A CULINARY JOURNEY

MY GO - TO DISHES

PIZZA

Pizza comes in a very big variety of tastes depending on ingredients, making, size, and texture. Whether it's the crispy thin crust of a Margherita or the deep-dish of a Chicago-style pizza, each bite promises a burst of flavors. Toppings range from classic pepperoni and cheese to gourmet options like truffles and arugula, making pizza a versatile and universally loved dish.

FRIED CHICKEN

Fried chicken is a beloved comfort food that varies by region. Each country has its unique seasoning and preferred cut of chicken, but the base flavor remains universally delicious. From the spicy kick of Korean fried chicken to the buttermilk tenderness of Southern American style, fried chicken is crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, perfect for any occasion.

RAMEN

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish known for its rich and flavorful broth. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a broth, with common flavors including soy sauce and miso. Toppings such as sliced pork (chāshū), nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and scallions add depth to the dish. Ramen is a perfect blend of textures and flavors, making it a hearty and satisfying meal.

FRIED RICE

Fried rice is a delightful dish that combines rice with a medley of vegetables, proteins, and savory seasonings. Whether it's the classic Chinese-style fried rice with soy sauce and eggs, or the spicy Thai basil fried rice, each version offers a unique twist. The beauty of fried rice lies in its versatility and the ability to turn leftover rice into a delicious and satisfying meal. The sizzling sound of the rice hitting the hot wok, the vibrant colors of the veggies, and the aroma of the seasonings make fried rice an exciting culinary experience every time.
